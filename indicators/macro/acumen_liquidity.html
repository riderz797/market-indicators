Construction (Howell Methodology)
1CB Liquidity = Fed (WALCL − TGA − RRP) + ECB + BoJ + China FX
2Private Liquidity = US Bank Credit (H.8)
3Gross Global Liquidity = CB + Private
4Drains: −3% per +$10/bbl oil • −4% per +10 MOVE pts • −10% per +10% DXY (vs 2yr baseline, capped ±30%)
5Net Liquidity = Gross × (1 + drain %)
6Indicator = 3-month MA of YoY % change in Net Liquidity

Both indicator and benchmark are converted to z-scores (2015–present mean/std) before plotting. The liquidity series is then time-shifted 195 days forward to show its predictive signal for equities.
